# AIPE Labs

> Power Electronics AI Agent — from device to converter to grid. This file is the
> agent-readable index of everything AIPE Labs publishes: the skills, packages,
> and agents we build on GitHub, plus the databases, design references, and case
> studies on this site. Point your AI coding agent (Claude Code, Codex, etc.) at
> this file and it can navigate all of it.

## Skills, Packages & Agents (GitHub)

- [AIPE Labs on GitHub](https://github.com/FulongLi): Home of our power electronics packages, skills, and agents — in active development. New skills are published here first.
- [Spirit-Connect-AIPE-Labs](https://github.com/FulongLi/Spirit-Connect-AIPE-Labs): Source of this website, including this index file.

## Power Electronics

- [Devices](https://aipel.co.uk/power/devices/): Semiconductor device testing, modelling, and characterisation (SiC, GaN, Si) — electrical and thermal characterisation for design automation
- [Device Characterisation](https://aipel.co.uk/power/characterizations/): Switching characteristics, switching losses, thermal resistance, and junction temperature analysis
- [Converters](https://aipel.co.uk/power/converters/): Converter topologies, magnetics sizing, control synthesis, and multi-objective optimization (LLC, DAB, multi-level DC-AC, interleaved buck/boost)
- [Microgrids](https://aipel.co.uk/power/microgrids/): Microgrid architectures, DC distribution networks, and hybrid AC/DC power systems
